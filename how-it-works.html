How it Works

A structured request-to-delivery workflow for acquiring and selling premium tickets — designed for speed, clarity, and clean execution.

Acquire Tickets

Three steps to your seats.

Tell us what you want, and our network does the rest. From sourcing to secure delivery, the process is built for simplicity.

1

Request

Share the event, city, date, seating preferences, party size, and budget. Be as specific or open as you'd like.

2

Curate

We source options through verified channels and relationships, then provide a clean shortlist with clear tradeoffs.

3

Execute

You confirm; we coordinate secure mobile transfer (typically via Ticketmaster/SeatGeek depending on the venue).

For detailed questions on pricing, delivery, resale, and scope — visit the FAQ page.

Sell Tickets

Plans change. We make it easy.

If you're looking to offload tickets — whether it's a single game or an ongoing inventory — CubLion can help coordinate resale and reallocation through our network and trusted channels.

List With Us

Share your inventory details — event, section, row, number of seats, and your target price.

We Match Buyers

Our network connects your tickets to verified buyers who are actively looking for premium access.

Clean Exit

We handle transfer logistics and coordination — so you can exit cleanly without wasting valuable time.
