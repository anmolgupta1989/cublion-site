FAQs

What events do you focus on?

We primarily focus on major U.S. sports — NBA, NFL, MLB, and NHL — while expanding into concerts, MLS, and other live events depending on market availability. If you have a specific venue or event in mind, reach out and we’ll confirm.

Who do you work with?

We serve small and mid-sized businesses, high-net-worth individuals, family offices, private groups, and also individuals looking for a single game solution. Party size and budget vary — our job is to curate options and execute cleanly.

How are tickets delivered?

Tickets are typically delivered via secure mobile transfer to your email or phone number through the venue’s primary ticketing platform (often Ticketmaster or SeatGeek, depending on the event).

How quickly do you respond?

We aim to respond quickly and commit to an initial response within 24 hours. Many requests are handled sooner, depending on timing and complexity.

How does pricing work?

Pricing depends on demand, seating tier, timing, and availability. When possible, our sourcing methods can align pricing with strong value relative to market conditions, including scenarios where access can resemble season-level pricing. We present clean options with clear tradeoffs so you can decide efficiently.

Do I need to commit to season tickets?

No. Our model is designed to provide premium access without requiring full-season ownership. You can request access event-by-event, or coordinate ongoing planning.

Is CubLion a resale marketplace?

CubLion is not a public marketplace. We operate as an access and execution platform, coordinating sourcing, allocation, and delivery through verified channels.
