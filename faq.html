FAQ

Detailed answers on supported leagues, who we serve, ticket delivery, response times, pricing, and resale support.

What events do you focus on?

We focus primarily on major American sports — NBA, NFL, MLB, and NHL. We are constantly expanding our network into concerts, MLS, and other live events. If you have questions about a specific event, venue, or city, please reach out via the Contact page.

Who do you work with?

We serve a wide range of clients — from small and mid-sized businesses to high-net-worth individuals, family offices, and private groups. We also work with individuals looking for a single game solution. No customer is "too small" — party size and budgets vary, and we'll work with you regardless of the request when possible.

How are tickets delivered?

Tickets are typically delivered directly to your email or phone number via secure mobile transfer. Most sporting events are hosted through major ticketing platforms (often Ticketmaster or SeatGeek depending on the venue). Provide your most accurate account/contact information and we'll handle delivery coordination.

How quickly do you respond?

We prioritize fast response times. We aim to respond within 24 hours, and many requests are handled sooner depending on timing, complexity, and availability.

How does pricing work?

Pricing is customizable and depends on the event, timing, and seating tier. In many instances, we're able to secure tickets at full-season level prices (i.e., face value) or less. This is driven by our ability to leverage long-standing relationships and network access across major leagues, and by coordinating inventory efficiently. When we present options, we include clear tradeoffs so you can choose quickly with confidence.

Do you help clients sell tickets too?

Yes. CubLion supports both sides of the marketplace: acquiring tickets and helping clients offload tickets when plans change. Resale support can include coordinating buyer interest within networks and using appropriate external channels when necessary.

Is CubLion a resale marketplace?

CubLion functions as a marketplace and execution platform — helping clients acquire premium access and coordinate selling when needed. We're not a "browse-and-click" public exchange; we operate through curated sourcing, structured coordination, and verified delivery.

Still have questions?

We're happy to help. Reach out and we'll get back to you quickly.
