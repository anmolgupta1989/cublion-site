The Front Row. Networked.

CubLion Entertainment is a premium access network for sports and select live events—built for busy professionals and businesses who want better seats, less friction, and a smarter way to buy and sell within a trusted ecosystem.

Built for Connection—Not Just Tickets.

CubLion started as a simple attempt to stay connected to sports during a demanding medical career. What grew from shared season access wasn’t just a better way to manage premium tickets—it was a community. The front row became a place where meaningful relationships formed and professional bonds strengthened.
