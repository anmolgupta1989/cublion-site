Our story

Built for Connection — Not Just Tickets.

CubLion started as a simple attempt to stay connected to sports during a demanding medical career. But what grew from shared season access wasn't just a better way to manage premium tickets — it was a community. The front row became a place where meaningful relationships formed, business bonds strengthened, and experiences were shared.

That's what we built CubLion to deliver: more time in better seats, less friction, and a trusted network that makes it easy to buy when you want to go — and sell when plans change.
