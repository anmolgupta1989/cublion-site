Our Story

CubLion began as a simple attempt to stay connected to the sports world—and evolved into a network designed to make premium access easier, more communal, and more meaningful.

How it started

CubLion began long before it became a business.

Growing up, access to professional sports wasn’t always easy. My father, an immigrant physician, worked tirelessly to build a life for our family. Time and resources were limited. But sports—the camaraderie, the competition, the shared emotional highs and lows—were always central to who I was.

I didn’t just love watching games. I loved what they created: connection, energy, community, and a sense of belonging.

The unexpected power of the front row

Years later, during medical residency, time became scarce again—but the desire to stay connected to sports never left. What began as occasionally borrowing tickets from a friend turned into something bigger: pooling resources, structuring season access intelligently, and figuring out how to make premium seating sustainable.

But something unexpected happened. The value wasn’t just in the seats. It was in what those seats created. Front row access—whether courtside, club level, or on the glass—isn’t just proximity to the game. It’s proximity to people. Conversations started. Friendships formed. Clients became long-term relationships. The experience became communal.

It wasn’t about status. It was about connection. That realization shaped CubLion.

Communalizing the front row

The financial engineering came later. We learned how to make premium access easier—without forcing full-season commitments, and without requiring clients to burn valuable time navigating the resale market.

What CubLion makes easier:
  • Accessing premium seats without a full-season commitment
  • Getting in at favorable pricing through a coordinated network
  • Reallocating or selling tickets when plans change—without the chaos
  • Reducing the time, friction, and uncertainty behind premium access

What we really deliver

We are not just a ticket marketplace. We are a network—built to help professionals, businesses, and individuals spend more time in better seats, celebrating the athletes you love, and making connections with like-minded people.

Life is about connection. About passion. About winning. We simply make it easier to be in the room—or better yet, in the front row.
