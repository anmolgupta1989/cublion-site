Our Story

Where It Started

Growing up, access to professional sports games wasn’t always easy. My father, an immigrant physician, and my mother worked tirelessly to build a life for our family. Time and resources were limited. Yet sports — the camaraderie, the competition, the shared emotional highs and lows — were always central to who I was.

Years later, during my medical training, time again became scarce. But the desire to stay connected to the sports world never left. What started as borrowing tickets from a friend turned into pooling resources with others in order to secure season tickets as a group.

What I didn’t expect was what happened next. Tickets became more than just seats. They became a social hub. A networking opportunity. A place where meaningful relationships formed. And a chance to rethink the entire sports experience.

What began as a practical solution to attend games became a platform for connection — strengthening friendships, building professional relationships, and supporting client experiences.

Figuring out how to make it work financially came later. The true value wasn’t arbitrage — it was accessibility. Over time, we built systems to manage allocation, coordinate resale, address exchanges, and simplify logistics.

Today, CubLion operates as a marketplace and execution layer, helping hundreds of clients acquire premium access to sporting events, while also utilizing their tickets when plans change.

  • Access premium seats without long-term commitments
  • Secure favorable pricing wherever possible
  • Exit positions cleanly without wasting valuable time
  • Avoid the chaos of timing and pricing the resale market
  • Form meaningful friendships and business connections

A Social Network for Professional Sports Enthusiasts

The goal is simple: spend more time in better seats. Celebrate the athletes you love. Build connections with like-minded professionals who value access, experience, and excellence.

Life is about connection. Passion. Winning. We make it easy.
