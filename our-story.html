Our Story

CubLion began as a desire to stay connected to the sports world — and evolved into a network built around access, connection, and the shared experience of being in the front row.

Where It Started

Growing up, access to professional sports wasn’t always easy. My father, an immigrant physician, worked tirelessly to build a life for our family. Time and resources were limited. But sports — the camaraderie, the competition, the shared emotional highs and lows — were always central to who I was.

I didn’t just love the game. I loved what it created — connection, energy, belonging. Being present. Being part of something bigger.

The Unexpected Opportunity

Years later, during medical residency, I found myself busy building a career — but still wanting to stay connected to sports in a meaningful way. What began as borrowing tickets from a friend turned into pooling resources with a few others to secure season tickets.

What I didn’t expect was what happened next. The front row became more than seats. It became a social hub. A networking opportunity. A place where meaningful relationships formed.

Clients became friends. Friends became business partners. Wins felt bigger. Losses felt shared. It wasn’t about status — it was about connection.

Attending Game 4 of the 2023 NBA Finals in Miami, FL with my dad.

Communalizing the Front Row

Figuring out how to make it work financially came later. The true value wasn’t arbitrage — it was accessibility.

We built a system that allows people to:

• Access premium seats without long-term commitments • Enter at favorable pricing when possible • Exit positions cleanly without wasting valuable time • Avoid the chaos of timing and pricing the resale market

The goal is simple: Spend more time in better seats. Celebrate the athletes you love. Build connections with like-minded people.

Life is about connection. Passion. Winning. We make it easy.
