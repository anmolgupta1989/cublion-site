Built from Personal Passion. Designed for Modern Professionals.

CubLion began long before it became a business.

Growing up, access to professional sports wasn’t always easy. My father, an immigrant physician, worked tirelessly to build a life for our family. Time and resources were limited. Yet sports — the camaraderie, the tension of competition, the shared emotional highs and lows — were always central to who I was.

Years later, during medical residency, time became scarce again. But the desire to stay connected to the sports world never left. What started as pooling resources for season access evolved into something more structured and scalable.

From Shared Access to Structured Platform

What began as a practical solution to attend games became a platform for connection — strengthening friendships, building professional relationships, and supporting meaningful client experiences.

Over time, we built systems to manage allocation, resale coordination, exchanges, and logistics efficiently. Today, CubLion provides curated, structured access without requiring full-season ownership from clients.

Why It Matters

CubLion operates at the intersection of discipline and passion — combining structured execution with a lifelong love of sports. Built while practicing medicine full-time, the platform reflects thoughtful growth, careful coordination, and long-term relationship building.

This is not about hype. It’s about building something durable, scalable, and meaningful.
